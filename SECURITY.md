## Important Notice

Ozentime AV is a **new antivirus company product** under active development.  
- We focus on delivering protection against **advanced threats** using heuristic analysis and AI redefinitions.  
- We do **not guarantee 100% security**. As our AVs are still evolving, detection coverage may be incomplete.  
- Until Ozentime AV 2.0 is fully matured, we **advise users not to rely solely on Ozentime AV** for complete protection.  
- We recommend using Ozentime AV alongside other antivirus solutions if needed, to ensure layered security.  

Please keep this in mind when downloading and using our software.
